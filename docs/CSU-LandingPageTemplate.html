BenefitHub

CSU is proud to offer BenefitHub members and their immediate family members a 10% tuition discount.

Learning Partner members and employees are eligible to receive these exclusive benefits, as well as their spouses and children. Affordable tuition rates coupled with various ways to save provides recipients with a unique opportunity to achieve your academic goals within your budget and on your own terms.

With all degree programs offered completely online, there is no need for students to miss valuable time at work to attend a class or take a test. Our programs give students the flexibility to fit their education around their career and family commitments.
